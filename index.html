Let Me Introduce My Self

Hello Everyone! I'm Ali Mzayhem and I'm currently working in Baabda, Mount Lebanon where I started my personal brand @alithedesignr and for those of you who don't know; alithedesignr is an online hub where design enthusiasts gather to share, learn, and create. But let me walk you through my journey which started in 2013, the year I became the UCMAS Mental Math National Champion in Lebanon, which lead me to represent Lebanon on Malaysia's international stage. After that, the momentum kept going as in 2014 I earned the title of first runner-up in the UCMAS Mental Math National Championship.

These and many other experiences all influence my brand today as i specialize in crafting eye-catching user interfaces and turning design dreams into reality which also made me expand my horizons to the world of e-commerce, especially with Shopify, where I proudly serve as a trusted partner.

Beyond the technicalities, I'm driven by the joy of problem-solving and the thrill of perfecting the tiniest details.

Finally, my journey is far from being over, for I always have room for developing & designing and am looking forward to all the possibilities that lie ahead.
